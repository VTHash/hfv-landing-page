Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

We do not collect personal information unless voluntarily provided. Wallet connections only access your public address.

When you connect a wallet, only your public wallet address is accessed. We never collect or store private keys.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We may use minimal analytics tools to monitor usage. These tools do not collect personally identifiable data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site may integrate trusted third-party services (e.g., hosting, analytics) with their own privacy practices.

5. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure. Use of the site is at your own discretion.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Revisions will be posted on this page with the updated date.

7. Contact

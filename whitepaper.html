🔹 Staking Mechanism

Lock Options: 21 Days, 3 Months, 6 Months, 12 Months

Reward Formula:

HFV Amount × 0.4 × 17.38 × Duration Multiplier

Where:
- 0.4 is the base reward rate
- 17.38 = 365 / 21 (annual multiplier)
- Multiplier: 1 (21 Days), 3, 6, or 12

Example: Staking 120 HFV for 3 months:
120 × 0.4 × 17.38 × 3 = 2,503.68 HFV

🔹 Managed Funds (MF) & PSF

  • All transfer fees go to Managed Funds (MF).
  • From MF, funds are directed to PSF (Protection Security Fund).
  • PSF and MF wallets are locked and publicly viewable.
  • 15% of tokens permanently locked from ICO start.

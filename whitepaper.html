HFV Token Whitepaper

Token Overview

Name: HFV Token

Total Supply: 70,000,000 HFV

Token Distribution

  • 3% – Private Sale
  • 18% – Presale
  • 15% – PSF (Protection Security Funds)
  • 18% – Team and Developers
  • 15% – Airdrop
  • 31% – ICO and Exchange
  • 13% – Staking Mechanism
  • 30–40% – Strategic Sale
  • 10% – Airdrops for holders
  • 10% – Crosschains

Staking Mechanism

Hold to Earn: Rewards increase the longer you stake.

Options: 3, 6, 12 month locks

Reward Formula:

HFV × 0.4 × 17.38 × Duration Multiplier

Example: 120 HFV × 0.4 × 17.38 × 6 = 5012.16 HFV

Managed Funds (MF)

All transaction fees go to MF. From MF, funds flow to PSF.

App development, team allocation, and marketing are locked.

15% of tokens locked at ICO launch.

Key Concepts

  • PSF (Protection Security Funds): Receives fees, protects liquidity
  • Team Vesting: 1 year lock; 2% released monthly post-lock

Future DAO Proposals

  • Investment DAOs: Collective fund management
  • Grant DAOs: Funding aligned community projects
  • Social DAOs: Organizing shared interest communities
  • Platform DAOs: Governing protocols/platforms transparently
